Get to School 5

About Page

Get to School 5 is an ambitious project that merges the captivating world of the anime "Bleach" with the everyday challenge of getting to school. This page serves as a hub for updates on the game's development. Expect to find regular status updates and insights into the creative process. If you're curious to learn more beyond what's provided, don't hesitate to reach out and inquire further.

Game Page

The Game Page hosts the current iteration of the game for testing purposes. As a work in progress, Get to School 5 is continuously evolving. While the game may still be in its nascent stages, progress is steady, with many exciting features yet to be implemented. Consider this just the initial phase of an adventure waiting to unfold.
